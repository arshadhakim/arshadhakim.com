Today's gender is a forest fire

arshad hakim draws and makes films.
he despises writing a bio and at best he thinks of it as a game that he wants to play.

“gender of the day” is a genderbot that let's us outsource our feelings about gender to a bot, which i think should free up a lot of mental space for other things.

the game works as a list. a sentence that begins with: the gender of the day is …. or today’s gender is ….

few examples:

  • the gender of the day is a shimmering nebulous black hole.
  • today’s gender is the sound of the static between radio stations.
  • the gender of the day is a spectral moon.
  • the gender today is the smell of patchouli and sound of thunder in the distance.
  • today’s gender is brave and preoccupied.
  • today’s gender is a hurricane.
